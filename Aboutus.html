Who We Are

Swapthrough Impex Pvt. Ltd. is a Bengaluru-based export company founded with a clear vision: to introduce the richness of India’s handcrafted and natural products to global markets with transparency, care, and consistency. As a growing enterprise, we work closely with skilled artisans, weavers, small manufacturers, and reliable farms to curate products that represent genuine Indian craftsmanship and quality.


Our developing product portfolio includes jute products, carpets, leather goods, textiles, toys, handicrafts, and premium coffee beans. Each item is carefully sourced and reviewed to align with international quality expectations, ensuring dependable supply while preserving traditional techniques and natural integrity.

What We Do..

Ethical sourcing and sustainability are central to our approach. We prioritize responsible production practices that support artisan communities and environmentally conscious manufacturing, creating value for both producers and buyers.

In addition to product sourcing, Swapthrough Impex provides essential export support services, including custom sourcing, packaging coordination, and logistics assistance. This integrated approach helps international retailers, wholesalers, and distributors engage with Indian suppliers smoothly and efficiently.

Our Vission And Mission
